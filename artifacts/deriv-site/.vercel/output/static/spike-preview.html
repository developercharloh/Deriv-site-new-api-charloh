How it works

⚡ Spike Detector — What it does

Detection: Scans the same 1,000 tick prices the orb already fetches. Calculates the rolling standard deviation of price changes. Any jump > 3σ from the mean = spike flag.

Blocking: If a spike occurred in the last 60 seconds on any market, signal generation is blocked for that market. Other clean markets can still produce signals.

Countdown: A cooldown bar shows how long until the spike clears. Auto-unlocks when market is stable again.

Orb badge: Orange pulsing badge = spike active. Green = all clear. The ⚡ icon itself turns orange so you notice it instantly even when the panel is closed.

No false positives: Only flags statistically significant jumps — normal market noise won't trigger it. Boom/Crash indices have a wider threshold since they spike by design.
